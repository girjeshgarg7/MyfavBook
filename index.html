The power of subconsious mind

Positive, Effective,motivating is a 1963 novel by Joseph Murphy.

'The power of your subconscious mind', the author fuses his spiritual wisdom and scientific research to bring to light how the sub-conscious mind can be a major influence on our daily lives.

Joseph Murphy(born 20 May 1898) was an Irish-born American author. His most famous
work The Power Of Subconious mind, became an immediate bestseller.
